# Traditional AI Domains — Deep Theory Tracks

These notebooks provide the mathematical and theoretical foundations behind the tools used in the main curriculum (Notebooks 01-35).

## Reading Order

Complete the **main curriculum** (NB01-34) first for production skills, then use these tracks to deepen your theoretical understanding.

## Tracks

### Machine Learning (ML/) — 9 Notebooks
Mathematical foundations of classical ML: linear algebra, optimization, tree models, kernels, unsupervised learning, evaluation, and system design.

### Deep Learning (DL/) — 7 Notebooks  
Neural network mathematics from scratch: backpropagation, activations, initialization, optimizers, regularization, compute hardware, and **Transformer architecture**.

### Computer Vision (CV/) — 17 Notebooks
The complete modern CV stack: CNN/RepVGG, RT-DETR, Mask2Former, Vision Transformers & SSMs (Vision Mamba), Contrastive Learning (DINOv2/I-JEPA), 3D Gaussian Splatting, Diffusion Transformers (DiT), Multimodal VLA Models, and Tracking/Pose/OCR modules.

### Natural Language Processing (NLP/) — 6 Notebooks
Text preprocessing, tokenization (BPE), word embeddings (Word2Vec), RNNs/LSTMs, attention mechanism, pre-training (BERT/GPT), and HuggingFace pipelines.

### Reinforcement Learning (RL/) — 6 Notebooks
MDP foundations & Bellman equation, Q-Learning & TD methods, Deep Q-Networks (DQN), Policy Gradient (REINFORCE & Actor-Critic), **PPO**, and **RLHF alignment** — the complete path from MDPs to ChatGPT training.

### Recommender Systems (RS/) — 4 Notebooks
Content-based & collaborative filtering, matrix factorization & embeddings, deep learning (Neural CF, Two-Tower), and **production systems** (ANN retrieval, bandits, diversity, system design).

### Time Series (TS/) — 4 Notebooks **[NEW]**
Classical forecasting (ARIMA, ETS, Prophet), ML feature engineering (lags, rolling stats, XGBoost), deep learning (LSTM, TCN, Transformer, **Chronos/TimesFM foundation models**), and **production systems** (anomaly detection, streaming architecture, system design).

## Pedagogical Format

Every notebook includes:
- **Socratic Deep Check** — A thought-provoking question answered by the end
- **Junior to Senior: Concept Bridge** — Production perspective, mental model, common pitfall
- **Knowledge Check** — Quiz questions with hidden answers
- **Practical Practice** — Coding exercises with solutions
- **Cross-references** — Links to relevant main curriculum notebooks
